This program is designed to simulate the motion of the classical N-body problem.

Click "start" and then "resume" button to run a simulation. More Instruction.

Simulation One: Pentagon-Star type for the paper titled "Star pentagon and many stable choreographic solutions of the Newtonian $4$-body problem" by T. Ouyang and Z. Xie. arxiv

Simulation Two:  Pentagon-Star type solutions for unequal masses in the paper "A continuum of periodic solutions to the planar four-body problem with various choices of masses" by T. Ouyang and Z. Xie (arxiv). The simulations for some other type of solutions also include here.

Simulation Three: Some interesting orbits. More updates come soon.
